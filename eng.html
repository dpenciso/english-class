Free Virtual English Tutoring

About...

This free English tutoring service is for Spanish speakers and is being offered by David Enciso, a Peruvian student at Utah Valley University. This tutoring is meant to help those who are wanting to learn English for the first time, or even expand their current English vocabulary. David has spoken Spanish since he could first talk and has taken high school and univeristy courses as well as lived in Chile for one year and visited Peru more than 20 times.

David wants to give back to the community during COVID-19 by teaching English classes online through Zoom. These sessions will be first come first serve so sign up today!
